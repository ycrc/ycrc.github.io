## October 2025


### Intro to HPC YouTube

In September, we offered two Introduction to High Performance Computing workshops focused on Yale's clusters. These hands-on sessions welcomed 60 new cluster users. Thank you to everyone who attended! If you missed the live training, you can watch the recorded version on our YouTube channel: [https://research.computing.yale.edu/intro-to-hpc](https://research.computing.yale.edu/intro-to-hpc)

### Bouchet Info Session Recording Available

In this session, Senior Computational Research Support Analyst, Tom Langford, Ph.D., explains the key differences between our legacy clusters and Bouchet, and provides guidance to help users transition successfully. [Access the recording on YCRC's YouTube Channel](https://youtu.be/GZ7_bDhPXqU).

We encourage all users to transition to Bouchet to take advantage of its powerful computing resources. The cluster is built with our newest and fastest computing resources, currently featuring:

- 11,000 CPUs
- 128 GPUs, including 80 H200 cards

To learn more about Bouchet and request access, visit [https://docs.ycrc.yale.edu/clusters/bouchet/](/clusters/bouchet/)
